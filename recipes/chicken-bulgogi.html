Chicken Bulgogi

Description

Chicken Bulgogi is an easy and quick Korean BBQ chicken recipe made with gochujang. You can grill this slightly sweet and savory chicken, broil it in the oven, or pan-fry it. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Chicken Bulgogi can be made with soy sauce based seasoning, which has its own unique savory flavor.

Ingredients

    Boneless skinless chicken thigh
    Chicken bulgogi (Korean BBQ chicken) sauce

Korean Barbecue Sauce Ingredients:

    gochujang
    gochugaru
    soy sauce
    light brown sugar
    Korean corn syrup
    garlic
    ginger puree
    sesame oil
